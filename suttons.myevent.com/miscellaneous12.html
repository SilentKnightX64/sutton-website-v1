GREETINGS FAMILY!

YOUNG PEOPLE TALENT SEARCH

The 2019 Sutton Family Reunion – Detroit is quickly approaching and we are so excited to be planning to host you all here in the heart of downtown Detroit! The Banquet Committee is working to solidify the program for this year’s Bi-Annual Celebration entitled L.E.G.A.C.Y. and would like your help! This year’s theme is LEGACY and we simply cannot complete our program without getting our amazing youth involved!

The 2019 SFR Planning Committee would like any attending parents, aunts, uncles or grandparents, if you have a young one (ages 5 – 17) who is talented and would like to share that talent with the family by performing in this year’s banquet program, please let us know ASAP! All performance talent welcome! Singing, dancing, playing an instrument, drawing, painting or spoken word and poetry for example! If a little one in your family would be interested, please submit their full name, age and talent to SFRDetroit2019@gmail.com. Please be sure to include an adult contact for the child, as well as that adult’s contact information (phone and email address).

We know we have some amazingly talented and divinely gifted young people in our family and we want to lift them in encouragement and give them room to shine! There are no limits to the amount of children who can be submitted for consideration so please discuss this opportunity with your young ones and submit any and all names of those who would like the chance to bless the family with their special light. Once we have all submissions, both the youth and the adult contact will be notified of next steps. All performers will have photo and bios featured on the family website www.suttons.myevent.com As well as be listed in the LEGACY Banquet program!

 

Let’s get excited for another chance to fellowship together and lift each other up in love! Please have the requested information for interested parties submitted ASAP. Submissions will remain open until May 31st, 2019, or until slot(s) have been filled, whichever comes first.
