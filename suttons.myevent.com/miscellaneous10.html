From The Sutton Family President
 

My family,  it’s been a pleasure serving you over these past 9 years.  

We’ve come from very humble beginnings to an organized running family over the past 9 years where I’ve  received various accolades from others outside of our family,  as well as from family members.   

We are better organized and most importantly, over all, how well we execute family business and reunions.

 


We’ve, over the past 9  years  have achieved: 

Instituting By-Laws,
Streamed line our business meetings and practices, 
A fully functional Executive Board, 
Real time communications, 
First time ever having  a reunion in Detroit, 
Incorporated “Direct Descendent Dues”, 
Formulated  a process to vent issues and concerns, 
Established a Counsel of Elders, 
Actually raised over $1500 for our scholarship fund, 
Distributed $400 in Scholarships monies for the very first time.  And, 
Created , adopted  and approved our first ever Family Crest.  

 

Although I may not be the best President our family has had, or may not be considered the best in the future, but I was the President of this family at a time, when we needed strong leadership to move past the pass, bridge gaps, and set the family on course for even bigger and better things to come. 

 


We have a lot of talent in our family.   An Attorney, Doctors of Medicines and Ph.D.’s.  Very successful business owners,  professionals, Assistant  Deputy Director in Public Service,  Certified RN’s, s Certified Architect, Vice Principal in public school systems and Teachers. A Psychiatrist  and numerous other skilled and well educated Uncles, Aunties Nephews Nieces  and Cousins which has propelled those being raised up underneath us, future generations  to reach for the moon. 

We have all of this in just our family alone. All we need to do is draw on each others strength’s, openly communicate with one another, and most importantly, keep God first in all that we say and do and there’s no limit to what we as a family can accomplish. 

 


Again it’s been my pleasure to serve.  I love this family in a special way.  Regardless of the disagreements and the like, I just Simply Love This Family. 

(This is were I cry). 

 


I hope I’ve made decisions  based on the whole opposed to the few. My focus was to set us up to succeed and then turn things over to the very smart and intelligent  relatives  coming up, and  have the ability to take this family to another  level.  

 


I would also like to thank my  Mother,  (Aunt Queenie)  for all of your encouragement support and guidance through some challenging times for me personally,  and in dealing with family business,  still supporting me even when my decisions differed from yours. 

Thank you mother,  for being my mother. 

 


I would also like to thank Uncle Thomas, God  rest his soul, for being a mentor, and a wise counselor when called upon with nonpartial advice and correction.

 


Last but not least, I would like to thank my Executive Board members for being open, confidential when needed, decisive and very supportive when hard decisions had to be made and always talking in one voice. 

 


So I thank you family for allowing me to serve, for such a time as this, and always, always 

 


Acknowledge Him, and He shell direct our path.  

 


Thank You 

Larry D. Sutton 

Sutton Family Reunion President 

2015 - 2024
