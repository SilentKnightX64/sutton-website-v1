Greetings Family! 
 
We hope everyone is safe and well! It is with great honor that I introduce myself, Breniss Duffy, as the 2024 Sutton Family Reunion, Planning Committee Chairperson, for Mississippi. My esteemed committee members are: Larry Sutton, Vice Chairperson; Latice Sutton, Secretary; Erin Ward, Treasurer; and Monica Sutton, Assistant Treasurer. We are excited to plan our Family Reunion to commemorate this momentous occasion. 
 
As we come together to celebrate our 54th family reunion – to look back on our humble beginnings, celebrate our family lineage and heritage, and express our love and gratitude to our ancestors – we can’t do it without your participation. 
 
Your Mississippi Planning Committee is overjoyed to share information about our 2024 Reunion in the upcoming weeks! Our Family Reunion is an honor for us to organize and host, so we’re planning a weekend jam-packed with activities and entertainment for EVERYONE! Tell your parents, grandma, grandpa, brothers, sisters, cousins and every family member you know to be on the lookout for information to come on our 2024 reunion. 
 
We look forward to celebrating with you in Mississippi! 
 
Warmest Regards, 
 
Breniss Duffy
